Repository For Families (RFF)

What is it?

RFF is a platform for families to communicate with each other in a productive manner.

Share resources

RFF enables family members to put up shared resources for each member to use. Shared resources are available for members to reserve and attach to specific events. If any conflict arises then the two parties involved can barter for who gets control over the resource for that particular day. The resource owner is the one who makes the ultimate decision.

Share schedules

RFF enables members to post schedules so everyone is on the same page about what is going on.

What is it not?

RFF is simply a tool that families who already have good communication skills can add to their belt. It is not meant to be used as a way to control any persons life.
